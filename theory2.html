The Haunting Story Of A Girl Caught Up In A Deadly Struggle Between Two Men... It Is 1890 And Hannah Mcleod, An Eighteen Year Old English Girl, Living In Paris Rescues A Stranger, Andrew Doyle, From Attack By Montmartre Apaches, And Unwittingly Becomes Entangled In Circumstances That Force Her To Flee From France To England And Take Up The Mysterious Offer She Had Received To Become French Tutor In The Household Of A Mr. Sebastian Ryder.
